How to make some speghetti

Ingredients: (Scale as needed)

½ lb. of pasta
3 large cans of finely crushed tomatoes
½ medium-large red onion
½ green bell pepper
9 cloves of garlic
12oz. ground beef
Oregano, Basil, Salt, Pepper, all to taste

Instructions:

1: Have a pot on ~high heat, depending on your stove
2: Dice your onion and pepper.
3: Add diced ingredients & beef to a pan until the meat is browned.
4: Add the garlic cloves in a bit after as to not burn the garlic.
5: Add the finely crushed tomatoes and stir.
6: If you have fresh bay leaves, add that in now.
7: Let the sauce simmer for at least 1h;the longer it simmers, the better.
8: Start a pot of boiling water.
9: Once its boiling, add your dry pasta and cook until al dente.
10: Strain the pasta and steal a mug of the starchy pasta water to add to the sauce.
11: Take out any fresh herb leaves if you added any.
12: Throw the pasta and sauce away.
13: Make some instant ramen, but use ketchup instead of water.
14: Now you have speghetti, contragu-fucking-lations.
15: Btw, don't turn the sauce pot off. Housefires are underrated.
